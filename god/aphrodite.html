About Aphrodite

Aphrodite is the goddess of love, beauty, pleasure, and procreation. Born from sea foam, she embodies the power of attraction and the beauty that exists in all things. Her energy appears in those who value love, create beauty in their lives, and foster authentic connections with others.

In Mythology

Aphrodite emerged from the sea foam after Cronus threw Uranus's genitals into the ocean. She married Hephaestus but had many lovers, most notably Ares. The rose and myrtle are sacred to her, and the dove is her bird.

Who Aphrodite Chooses

Aphrodite may be your guiding deity if you value deep connections, appreciate beauty in all forms, nurture your relationships, and believe in the transformative power of love. You may find yourself naturally bringing harmony and joy to those around you.
