About Hermes

Hermes is the messenger of the gods, god of commerce, communication, and travel. With his winged sandals and staff, he moves swiftly between worlds. He represents the power of words, the joy of discovery, and the ability to adapt to any situation.

In Mythology

Born to Zeus and Maia, Hermes invented the lyre on his first day of life. As the divine messenger, he delivered messages between gods and mortals, guided souls to the underworld, and protected travelers. His staff, the caduceus, remains a symbol of communication and healing.

Who Hermes Chooses

Hermes may be your guiding deity if you're naturally quick-witted, love traveling and new experiences, excel at bringing people together, and adapt easily to change. You may find joy in the journey itself rather than just the destination.
