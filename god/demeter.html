About Demeter

Demeter is the goddess of agriculture, fertility, and motherly love. She teaches us that growth requires patience and care. Her energy appears in those who nurture others through life's cycles, provide for their communities, and understand that fallow periods are part of growth.

In Mythology

Demeter is the daughter of Cronus and Rhea. When her daughter Persephone was abducted by Hades, Demeter's grief caused the earth to wither. Their annual reunion brings spring and harvest, while Persephone's time in the underworld brings winter. This myth explains the changing seasons.

Who Demeter Chooses

Demeter may be your guiding deity if you find joy in nurturing others, understand the value of patience, care deeply about family and community, and accept that life has seasons of growth and rest. You may naturally provide for those around you.
