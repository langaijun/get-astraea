About Hebe

Hebe is the goddess of youth and the cupbearer of the gods. She represents eternal youthfulness, vitality, and the joy found in serving others. Her energy appears in those who maintain a youthful spirit regardless of age, find fulfillment in helping others, and bring fresh energy to any situation.

In Mythology

Hebe is the daughter of Zeus and Hera. She served nectar and ambrosia to the gods, keeping them forever youthful. After marrying Heracles, she was succeeded as cupbearer by Ganymede. Hebe represents the eternal spring of youth.

Who Hebe Chooses

Hebe may be your guiding deity if you maintain a light heart regardless of challenges, find joy in serving others, bring energy to any situation, and believe that youth is a state of mind. You may be someone others turn to when they need a lift.
