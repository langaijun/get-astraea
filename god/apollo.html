About Apollo

Apollo is the god of the sun, light, music, poetry, prophecy, and healing. He is the son of Zeus and Leto, twin brother of Artemis. Apollo represents the ideal of beauty, harmony, and intellectual achievement. In modern life, Apollo's energy appears in those who create art, seek truth, and bring warmth to others through their presence.

In Mythology

Born on the island of Delos, Apollo was a gifted archer and musician. He established his oracle at Delphi, one of the most important religious sites in ancient Greece. The Pythia, his priestess, delivered prophecies in his name. Apollo drove his chariot across the sky each day, bringing light to the world.

Who Apollo Chooses

Apollo may be your guiding deity if you feel drawn to artistic expression, have a sunny disposition even in difficult times, enjoy helping others heal and grow, and value truth and harmony. You may find yourself naturally bringing light and joy to those around you.
