About Iris

Iris is the goddess of the rainbow and messenger of the gods. She carries messages between Olympus and the mortal world, often bringing hope and good news. Her energy appears in those who connect people, communicate clearly, and maintain hope through life's transitions.

In Mythology

Iris is the daughter of Thaumas and Electra. She travels on her rainbow, carrying messages from the gods to humanity. As the goddess of the rainbow, she appears after storms as a sign of hope. She is also associated with the sea and sky.

Who Iris Chooses

Iris may be your guiding deity if you naturally connect people, communicate hope and positivity, see color where others see gray, and believe that things will work out. You may be the messenger who brings good news to those who need it most.
