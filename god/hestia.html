About Hestia

Hestia is the goddess of the hearth, home, and domestic life. She represents the quiet, steady warmth that makes a house a home. Unlike other gods who seek glory, Hestia found contentment in humble service. Her energy appears in those who create warmth wherever they go, not through grand gestures but through consistent care.

In Mythology

Hestia is the eldest daughter of Cronus and Rhea. She willingly gave up her seat on Mount Olympus to Dionysus, preferring the quiet life of tending the sacred fire. Every Greek home and city council maintained her eternal flame, symbolizing family continuity and community unity.

Who Hestia Chooses

Hestia may be your guiding deity if you naturally create comfort for others, find joy in simple acts of care, value stability over adventure, and have a quiet but powerful presence. You may be the one others turn to when they need to feel safe and grounded.
