About Persephone

Persephone is the queen of the underworld and goddess of spring growth. She embodies the journey through darkness into light, teaching that transformation requires both death and rebirth. Her energy appears in those who have faced deep challenges and emerged with renewed hope.

In Mythology

Persephone was abducted by Hades and became queen of the underworld. After her mother Demeter caused the earth to wither in grief, Zeus arranged for Persephone to spend half the year with Demeter (spring and summer) and half in the underworld (autumn and winter), creating the seasons.

Who Persephone Chooses

Persephone may be your guiding deity if you've experienced profound transformation, maintain hope through difficult times, understand that endings lead to new beginnings, and have depth in how you see the world. You may be someone who helps others find light in their darkness.
